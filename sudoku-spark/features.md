Sudoku Spark is built to be the most complete, most polished Sudoku experience on Android. Every feature is designed to help you learn, improve, and enjoy the world's most popular number puzzle.

## Daily Challenges

A new Sudoku puzzle is generated every single day. Daily challenges are the same for every player worldwide, so you can compare your time and strategy with friends. Complete them consecutively to build your daily streak — a powerful motivator for making brain training a daily habit.

- Fresh puzzle every day at midnight
- Streak tracking with best-streak records
- Fair play integrity — every player gets the same puzzle

## 4 Difficulty Levels

Sudoku Spark offers a natural progression path for every skill level:

| Level | Best For | What to Expect |
|-------|----------|----------------|
| **Easy** | Beginners and casual players | Plenty of given numbers; solvable with basic logic |
| **Medium** | Players learning new techniques | Requires scanning and basic elimination strategies |
| **Hard** | Intermediate to advanced players | Demands techniques like Naked Pairs and Pointing Pairs |
| **Expert** | Sudoku veterans seeking a challenge | Requires advanced strategies like X-Wing and Swordfish |

## Smart Hints

This is what sets Sudoku Spark apart. Most Sudoku apps just reveal the answer. Sudoku Spark explains **why** a number goes in a cell.

When you tap a hint, you learn the actual technique that solves that step — Naked Singles, Hidden Pairs, Box/Line Reduction, and more. Over time, you internalize these strategies and need fewer hints. You are not just solving; you are learning.

## Offline Play

No Wi-Fi? No problem. Sudoku Spark works completely offline. Every puzzle is generated and playable without an internet connection. Perfect for:

- Airplane flights
- Subway commutes
- Camping trips
- Anywhere with no signal

## Auto-Save

Close the app mid-puzzle and return later — your exact progress is preserved. Every number you enter, every pencil mark you make, and your elapsed time are saved automatically. You will never lose progress.

## 30+ Achievements

Stay motivated with an extensive achievement system spanning four tiers:

- **Bronze** — Getting started milestones
- **Silver** — Consistency and skill development
- **Gold** — Speed and difficulty mastery
- **Diamond** — Elite accomplishments for Sudoku masters

Achievements cover streak milestones, speed challenges, difficulty completion, and special goals.

## Detailed Statistics

Track your improvement over time with per-difficulty stats:

- Win rate percentage
- Best completion time
- Average completion time
- Total games played and completed
- Current and best streaks

## Notes Mode (Pencil Marks)

Track candidate numbers in each cell using pencil marks, just like solving on paper. Toggle notes mode with a single tap. Essential for Hard and Expert puzzles where tracking elimination is key.

## Unlimited Undo and Redo

Made a mistake? Tap undo. Changed your mind? Tap redo. There is no limit to how many moves you can undo, making the solving experience stress-free and exploratory.

## Duplicate Highlighting

Enter a conflicting number and it is immediately highlighted. This visual safety net helps you catch errors before they cascade across the grid, saving time and frustration.

## Premium Themes

Choose the visual style that suits your environment:

- **Light** — Clean and bright for daytime play
- **Dark** — Easy on the eyes for night sessions
- **Soft** — Warm tones for a relaxed solving experience

## Share Your Wins

Completed a challenging puzzle? Generate a victory card with your stats — time, difficulty, streak — and share it directly to social media or messaging apps. Challenge your friends to beat your time.
