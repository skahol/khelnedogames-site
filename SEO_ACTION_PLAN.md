## WEEK 2-4: Backlink Building

### Quora (High Authority, Free)
- [ ] Create a Quora profile for Khelnedo Games
- [ ] Answer 10+ questions with genuine value, linking to your guides:
  - "What is the best free Sudoku app?" → link to /sudoku-spark/best-sudoku-app/
  - "How do I play Sudoku?" → link to /sudoku-spark/how-to-play/
  - "What are Sudoku strategies?" → link to /sudoku-spark/tips-and-strategies/
  - "Is Sudoku good for the brain?" → link to /sudoku-spark/brain-training-games/
  - "Best Sudoku app for students?" → link to /sudoku-spark/sudoku-for-students/
- [ ] Write long, genuinely helpful answers (200+ words). Do NOT just drop links.

### Reddit (High Traffic, Careful Approach)
- [ ] Post in r/androidapps — "[Dev] Sudoku Spark: Free Sudoku app with hints that teach you techniques"

- [ ] IMPORTANT: Be transparent that you are the developer. Reddit hates hidden self-promotion.
- [ ] Engage with comments genuinely. One good Reddit post = hundreds of visitors.
