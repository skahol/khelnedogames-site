## Who We Are

Khelnedo Games is an independent game development studio founded by **Saksham Kahol**, a senior software engineer with a passion for building high-quality, thoughtfully designed puzzle games.

The name "Khelnedo" comes from Hindi — roughly translating to "let's play." It reflects our belief that games should be accessible, enjoyable, and genuinely beneficial for the mind.

## Our Mission

We build brain training puzzle games that respect the player. That means:

- **Teaching, not just testing** — Our games help you learn and improve, not just pass time
- **Privacy first** — We collect minimal data and never sell personal information
- **No paywalls** — All core features are free and accessible to everyone
- **Quality over quantity** — We focus on polished, world-class experiences rather than rushing to market

## Our Games

### Sudoku Spark — Play & Learn

Our flagship game brings the classic Sudoku puzzle to Android with a focus on learning. Smart Hints explain the logic behind every move, helping players internalize real solving techniques — from Naked Singles to X-Wing patterns.

**Key features:**
- Unlimited puzzles generated on-device with guaranteed unique solutions
- Smart Hints that teach techniques, not just answers
- Daily challenges with streak tracking
- 4 difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, Hard, Expert
- 30+ achievements across Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Diamond tiers
- Works fully offline with auto-save
- Light, Dark, and Soft themes

[Download Sudoku Spark on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.worldclass.sudoku)

## Our Approach to Game Development

Every game we build follows three principles:

1. **Designed for learning** — Games should make you better at something. Sudoku Spark's hint system teaches real techniques that transfer to solving harder puzzles independently.

2. **Built with modern standards** — We use the latest development practices and frameworks to deliver smooth, reliable, and beautiful experiences on any device.

3. **Respectful of your time and data** — No dark patterns, no aggressive monetization, no unnecessary data collection. Your attention and trust are not products we sell.
